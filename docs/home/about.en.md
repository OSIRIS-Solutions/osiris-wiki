# About OSIRIS

**OSIRIS** is an open, smart and intuitive research information system (RIS) that has been specially developed for smaller institutes, scientific institutions and research-related organisations. 

The system supports the **recording, management and evaluation of research activities** such as publications, third-party funded projects, lectures, software and courses. The focus is on ease of use, high flexibility and complete transparency.
